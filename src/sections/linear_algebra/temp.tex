Let $u,v$ be vectors of size $n$.
Let $\theta$ be the angle between the $u$ and $v$.

\textbf{Dot Product} of $u$ and $v$, 
\(
    u \dotp v 
    = \sum_{i = 1}^n u_i v_i
    = \magnitude{u} \magnitude{v} \cos{\theta}.
\).

If $u, v$ are of size $3$, then the \textbf{Cross Product} 
\(
    u \times v = 
    \begin{vmatrix}
        \vec{i} & \vec{j} & \vec{k} \\
        u_1 & u_2 & u_3 \\
        v_1 & v_2 & v_3
    \end{vmatrix}
\) 
where $u \times v$ is orthogonal to both $u$ and $v$,
and $\magnitude{u \times v} = \magnitude{u}\magnitude{b} \sin{\theta}$.

$proj_v (u) = \frac{u \dotp v}{\magnitude{v}^2} v$.
